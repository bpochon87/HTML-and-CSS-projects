Poems

Some thing

To be in such a wonderful position,
I have to question.
Is this existence something real?
Or am I just a transient ghost?
Crossing the ever expanding universe,
the cold, dark streams of the mind.
Something seems real here...
Some things, they seem real.

Must I Know?

Apparently there are more efficient ways to speak.
Must I know?
Evidently there is more money to be made.
Must I know?
Seemingly the universe can be contradicted.
Must I know?
Apparently I really don't know shit.
This, I must know.
