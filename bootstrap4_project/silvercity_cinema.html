| What Sets Us Apart |

Located at an elevation of 10,200', Leadville's Silver City Cinema has been entertaining families since 1968.

Our snack selection is second-to-none and we pride ourselves on being there for you if you need us.

Featuring DOLBY CINEMA (Atmos + Vision) paired with 60' screens to put you in the director's seat.

All of our facilities are handicap-accessible and feature accessories to limit your touch on surfaces.
